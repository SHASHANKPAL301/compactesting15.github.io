ANSELL

Ansell is a global leader in providing innovative safety solutions that protect people's health and well-being in various industries. Established in 1905 in Richmond, Australia, the company has a rich history of manufacturing and distributing a wide range of personal protective equipment (PPE), with a particular focus on gloves and other protective apparel. The company's commitment to research and development has led to the creation of high-quality, reliable, and ergonomic products that conform to stringent safety standards. Ansell continually strives to address emerging safety challenges and tailor solutions that cater to specific customer requirements. With a strong global presence and a vast distribution network, Ansell serves customers in over 55 countries, enabling them to access essential safety solutions efficiently. Their dedication to sustainability and responsible business practices underscores their efforts to create a safer, healthier, and more sustainable world.
